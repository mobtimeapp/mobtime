What is mobtime?

Mobtime is a real-time collaborative timer for mob programming.

Mobtime solves the problem of sharing a timer with a remote team.

By using the power of websockets and your hopes and dreams, you too can share a timer with your friends while you program.

Mobtime is open source, and you can find out more about it on github, and over on mrbarry.com.
